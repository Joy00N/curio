# Privacy Policy for Curio

**Last updated: February 25, 2026**

Yoonhee Jo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Curio mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored on Your Device

Curio stores the following data locally on your device using standard device storage:

- **Preferences**: Your selected interests, language, depth setting, and audio preferences
- **Learning History**: Topics you have viewed, including dates and content
- **Favorites**: Topics you have marked as favorites

This data is stored entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Information Sent to Our Servers

When you request an explanation for a custom topic (not pre-bundled with the app), the following is sent to our API server:

- The topic name you entered
- Your selected depth preference (light or normal)
- Your selected language preference

This information is used solely to generate educational content via OpenAI's API and is not stored on our servers after the response is returned.

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to generate educational content. When you request a custom topic explanation, the topic and preferences are sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy can be found at [https://openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking SDKs
- We do **not** serve advertisements
- We do **not** share or sell any data to third parties
- We do **not** create user accounts

## Data Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by going to Settings > Clear All Data within the app, or by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

Curio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains only educational content suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
